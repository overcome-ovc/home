Overcome Esports is excited to announce the official main roster for its EMEA division in Brawl Stars. This is a major step forward as the organization expands from its roots into one of the most competitive regions in global Brawl Stars esports. The new EMEA roster features three talented players:

[Arj] (https://x.com/arj2010241518) 
[Styx] (https://x.com/StyxBS)   
[Dusan] (https://x.com/Dusan126352) 

They will be supported by Head Coach Bearguba (@beargubagg) and Manager cc (@OVC_cc), whose expertise and guidance will help the team hit the ground running in the upcoming season.
This reveal marks the first half of Overcome’s plan to build a powerful presence in EMEA, with one more roster still to come. The organization is steadily putting together a lineup ready to compete at the highest level. The trio of Arj, Styx, and Dusan brings an exciting combination of sharp mechanics, smart game sense, and proven competitive experience. Together, they form a solid foundation that we believe will make waves in the EMEA scene.
